A Mercedes Benz GLC 250 model registration EX19 WSJ was hacked and stolen outside its owner's home in Burnside, Sawbridgeworth, early July 2021. The thieves used a scanning device aimed at the house to activate the ignition and drive away in the stolen car. Police have reported a rash of thefts of keyless cars in the Sawbridgeworth and Bishop's Stortford area, with seven stolen in only two weeks.
