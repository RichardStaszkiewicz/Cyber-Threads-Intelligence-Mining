Relay attack on keyless entry system of a vehicle. Two-Thief Attack: Attacker near the vehicle sends vehicle signal to attacker 2 who is near the car owner with the key. The response signal from the key is transmitted from attacker 2 to attacker 1 and the vehicle. Two-Thief Attack: As long as LF signal is used from vehicle to CID and RF signal is used from CID to vehicle, it is a deterministic attack that is possible with the help of suitable electronic devices.
