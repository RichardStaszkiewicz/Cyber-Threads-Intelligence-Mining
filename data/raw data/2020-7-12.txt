National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) stated they were victims of a ransomware attack on their email server. The ransomware operators breached NHAI servers and released sensitive data files. The ransomware operators claim to have uploaded 100% of the total data leak. The data leak included sensitive corporate operational documents such as the company's staff list, a passport copy of the former chairman of NHAI, details of dependent family members of NHAI employees, NHAI internal audit reports, and more.
