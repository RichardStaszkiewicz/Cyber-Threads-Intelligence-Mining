Seven vulnerabilities were found in EVlink City EVC1S22P4 / EVC1S7P4, EVlink Parking EVW2 / EVF2 / EVP2PE, and EVlink Smart Wallbox EVB1A. Among the potential impacts of the vulnerabilities, were allowing remote attackers to impersonate the user who manages the charging station or carry out actions on their behalf when crafted malicious parameters are submitted to the charging station web server and or impersonate the user or carry out actions on their behalf when crafted malicious parameters are submitted in POST requests sent to the charging station web server.
