Oakland county police arrested four people connected to the theft of four Dodge vehicles from a Highland Township dealership worth over $329,000. The four cars - a 2021 Dodge Durango Hellcat SRT, a 2021 Dodge Durango GT, a 2021 Dodge Ram TRX, and a 2017 Dodge Charger Hellcat SRT - were all stolen from Szott Dodge in Highland Township. Inside one of the located SUVs, the police found a computer device to reprogram key fobs.
