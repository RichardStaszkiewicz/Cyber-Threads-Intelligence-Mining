A vulnerability was found in a Qualcomm Snapdragon chip, a chip in use by OEMs to power in-vehicle infotainment and navigation as well as a 4G LTE modem for connectivity. The vulnerability, in which a race condition occurs while calling user space input-output-control from two different threads, can cause issues in video related to the infotainment system. The Snapdragon chip is used in many industries, including the automotive industry.
