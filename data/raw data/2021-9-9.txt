Forescout Research Labs, with support from Medigate Labs, discovered a set of 13 new vulnerabilities affecting the Nucleus TCP/IP stack, collectively called NUCLEUS:13. 13 new vulnerabilities were identified that allow for remote code execution, denial of service, and information leaks from the Nucleus TCP/IP stack. Discovered by Forescout Research Labs and Medigate Labs, Nucleus is used in safety-critical industries such as medical devices, automotive, and industrial systems. Siemens released patches for all reported vulnerabilities.
