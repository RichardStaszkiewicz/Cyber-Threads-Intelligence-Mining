Controlling functions like the brakes, acceleration, windshield wipers, etc.by adding a custom ECU which sends malicious messages to other ECUs. Replacing and integrating Electronic brake control module ( EBCM). Custom ECU recognized as valid network participant. This could lead to saftey-critical attacks, since the custom ECU can be modified to send malicious messages to the vehicle network, for example, to control the brakes, acceleration, windshield wipers, etc.
