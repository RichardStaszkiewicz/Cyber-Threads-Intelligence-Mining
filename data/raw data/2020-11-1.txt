Six vehicles were stolen from Twin Auto Sales, a Detroit-area car dealership through a keyless theft device. The thieves stole a black 2020 Dodge Durango SRT, a black 2017 Jeep Grand Cherokee SRT, a red 2014 Jeep Grand Cherokee SRT, a red 2020 Dodge Charger Scat Pack, a red 2018 Dodge Challenger R/T, and a black 2015 Dodge Challenger Hellcat. According to the dealership owner, the six vehicles were worth about $50,000 each.
