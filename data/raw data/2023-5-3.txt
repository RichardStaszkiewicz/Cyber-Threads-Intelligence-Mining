Several OEMs issued recalls for thousands of vehicles in the US during May 2023.  The recalls involve Mercedes-Benz, with 7,558 recalled vehicles, including the EQS sedans and S-Class sedans.  The recalls occured due to a malfunctioning electronic stability program ( ESP ) software, this software bug could disable various vehicle dynamics control systems, increasing the risk of a crash.  VinFast also recalled 999 of its 2023 VF8 EVs due to a software bug that can cause the display to go blank, preventing the driver from seeing critical safety information.  The company will release a software update to address the issue.
