Hackers in France figured out how to steal Peugeot, Lexus and Toyota vehicles in less than a minute, using a modified JBL bluetooth speaker to break into the vehicles. The discovery was made accidentally by Paris police authorities during an arrest of two suspected car hackers. The search for a stolen Peugeot 2008 revealed a JBL bluetooth speaker containing a quick start key that enabled the hackers to start the vehicle.
