Several vulnerbailities found in a Skoda Octavia vRS 2017 during a security investigation. Changing settings of Infotainment ECU to developer mode. Access to developer options in Infotainment ECU possible. Changing debugging destinations by accessing service mode, testmode, and engineering menu. Data can be stored on USB drive, developer information like network and phone logs, VW logs, vehicle states, etc. can be acquired.
