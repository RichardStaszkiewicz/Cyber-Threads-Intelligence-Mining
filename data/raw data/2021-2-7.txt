Analysts from Trend Micro rated DDoS attacks and electronic jamming as some of the highest cybersecurity risks for connected cars. Among the millions of endpoints in a connected car's ecosystem, analysts found 29 potential cybersecurity attack vectors and ranked five as the highest risks, which derived from the use of connected cars in satellite, cellular, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, RDS, eSIM-based telematics, and other types of connectivity to send and receive data.
