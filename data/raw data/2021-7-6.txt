A total of 124 vehicles were stolen in Oakville, Canada, between January 2021 and mid-July 2021. Sixty-six of the thefts involved relay or reprogramming technology. Residents reported 42 luxury vehicles stolen in the span of four weeks. Local police urged the community to take preventative steps and stay alert as they investigate these electronic thefts and confirmed the recent spike. Though the thieves stole most of the vehicles at night, they recently stole a number of cars in broad daylight. These thefts happened in less than a minute and targeted Lexus vehicles, Toyota models, Land Rovers, Range Rovers, and Ford pickup trucks.
