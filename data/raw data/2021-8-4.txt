Waterloo police reported that two dozen vehicles were stolen from driveways in Waterloo and Kitchener in one month. Waterloo Regional Police said Toyota and Lexus vehicles appeared to be the targets and issued a warning after four high-end vehicles were stolen in a single night in Waterloo using keyless entry and push-start technology vulnerabilities. The thefts have been occurring in the RIM Park area in Waterloo and in the neighbourhoods of Doon South and Grand River South in Kitchener.
