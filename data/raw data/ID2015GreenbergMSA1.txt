Man-in-the-Middle attack on GM OnStar RemoteLink App results in remote conttrol over vehicle functions like door locks and ignition. Attaching malicious device to target vehicle ( under chassis or bumper). Remote Wi-Fi connection can be interferred. Intercepting Wi-Fi communication between user and vehicle. User credentials can be acquired. Tracking vehicle location. Vehicle location is tracked, additionally personal information can be retrieved. Command spoofing to trigger vehicle functions. Unlocking doors, activating horn, starting the engine.
