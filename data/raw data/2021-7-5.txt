Thieves were shown stealing a luxury Mercedes vehicle in 60 seconds in the UK. Local police chiefs have warned owners of keyless cars of a spike in thefts by criminals using mobile gadgets to copy their electronic codes. Furthermore, between May and June, vehicle crime increased by 3.1% in the UK. It was reported that the thieves were using relay technology that dramatically improved the experience of drivers and also allowed criminals to exploit weaknesses in electronic security.
