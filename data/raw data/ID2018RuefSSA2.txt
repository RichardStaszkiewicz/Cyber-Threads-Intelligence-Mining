Daimler Mercedes-Benz COMAND 17/13.0 50.12 of Mercedes-Benz C-Class 2018 vehicles vulnerable for denial of service attacks through the navigation system. Defining or receiving a specific navigation route might cause the system to freeze and reboot after a few transmissions.  When the system next starts, it tries to re-calculate the route, which will cause a boot loop. Under certain circumstances, it is possible to quickly overwrite the malicious route to regain the stability of the system.
