Sniffing CAN messages with information ( speed) for cruise control. Sniffing the speed relevant CAN messages for Cruise Control. Relevant CAN messages with speed are identified. Spoofing of CAN messages with false information ( speed) to accelerate and decelerate the vehicle. Spoofing of CAN message with a lower speed of 40 km/h. Vehicle accelerates to 120 km/h which is against the drivers set cruise control speed. Spoofing of CAN messages with higher speed. Vehicle decelerates which is against the drivers set cruise control speed.
