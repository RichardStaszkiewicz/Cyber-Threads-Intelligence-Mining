A potential software bug affecting the electric motor of the Volkswagen ( VW) ID.4 electric vehicle led to a recall of 20,904 vehicles. The issue, which was discovered through customer complaints, could cause the motor to suddenly stop working while driving, posing a safety risk. VW issued a statement acknowledging the issue and is working on a solution, which may involve a software fix for the control unit software that controls the power output and torque of the motor. The company stated that only a small number of vehicles have been affected and that no accidents or injuries were reported in relation to the issue.
