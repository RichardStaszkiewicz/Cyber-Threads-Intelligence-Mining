A Range Rover vehicle was hacked into and stolen in Wolverhampton, UK, on March 9, 2021. CCTV footage revealed two thieves unlocking the keyless car using a wireless relay device, which received the keyfob signal from inside the owner's house and tricked the car into unlocking. The interior lights of the car turned on, as the thieves entered the vehicle, started the engine, and drove off with the stolen car. As of March 22, 2021, the car has not been found by police.
