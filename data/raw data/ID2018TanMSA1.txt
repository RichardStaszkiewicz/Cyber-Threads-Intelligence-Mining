Vehicle theft by using RollJam device to spoof the key fob. Jamming Key fob signal triggered by car driver. Car can not be opened by user. User presses button a second time. Both signals are intercepted by the attacker. The first signal is forwarded to the vehicle, which unlocks. The attacker now has the second signal whioch can be used to open the car, since that signal is still valid. Replay of valid key fob signal which was intercepted before. Car was unlocked.
