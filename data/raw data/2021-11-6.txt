Luxury cars such as Land Cruiser and Lexus were stolen one after another in Saitama Prefecture between January and August of 2021. Two of these thefts occurred using a CAN Invader, a Japanese term for a device car thieves use to hack into a car's controller area network ( CAN) bus, the internal network that connects all of its components. The CAN Invader method of hacking into vehicles conducts the thefts in a short time of about 10 minutes. The total number of car thefts from January to August was 277, 226 of which 226 were locked cars that were targeted. Land Cruisers were stolen in two places in Konosu City on September 23.
