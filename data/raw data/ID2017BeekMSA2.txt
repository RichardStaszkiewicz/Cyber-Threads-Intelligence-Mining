Telematic and Infotainment unit of Nissan Leaf compromised to establish a honeypot via its backend server to intercept connection attempts and GPS locations. Monitoring USB traffic of Telematic components. Data traffic was monitored which was being sent and received by outside components. Reverse engineering Telematics functionality. Buffer Overflow vulnerability was identified. Exploiting buffer overflow vulnerability. Telematics firmware was extracted. Reverse engineering the Telematics firmware. CAN messages can potentially be sent over the Telematics unit.
