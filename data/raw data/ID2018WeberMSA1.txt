Vgate iCar 2 WiFi OBD2 Dongles contain vulnerabilities which enable attackers to control safety-critical functions of a vehicle. Connecting to local OBD Dongle WLAN network. Data traffic of the OBD Dongle's WLAN connection can be read. Read WLAN data traffic from OBD Dongle. All data sent through WLAN can be read. Sensing malicious messages to the vehicle remotely. Malicous messages can be sent to the vehicle.
