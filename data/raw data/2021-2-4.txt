Ottawa police warned owners of high-end vehicles being stolen, following more than 200 reports related to thefts of newer-model Toyota and Lexus vehicles in 2020, according to the local police, and another 35 as of February 2021. The police added that most of the thefts happened overnight, between midnight and 5 am, using signal amplifiers in thefts of high-end vehicles with keyless entry technology in cities such as Toronto.
