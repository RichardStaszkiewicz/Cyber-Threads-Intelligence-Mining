USB Malware attack enables attackers to extract private data from the Infotainment system. Reverse Engineering Infotainment. Acquiring information about Infotainment functions. A maliciously crafted USB device plugged into a vehicle can infect the infotainment system with malware. Misusing SMS service to extract information. When a phone is connected to the infotainment system, the virus can read the phone's SMS and other private data.
