Approximately 80,000 vehicles were stolen in 2021 in Canada, according to Équité Association, an organization which analyzes auto thefts across Canada. According to investigators, thieves have become more organized and more reliant on sophisticated technologies. Keyless ignition systems continue to be targeted, including the Honda CR-V which was the most targeted vehicle in Ontario. Other commonly targeted vehicles included the Lexus RX 350, various Range Rover models, and the Toyota Highlander.
