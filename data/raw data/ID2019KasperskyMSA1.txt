During a security investigation of different third party tool like OBD Dongle, alarm systems, etc. several vulnerabilities were found. Connecting mobile device with OBD-dongle. Pairing between mobile device and OBD-Dongle possible without password. Connecting malicious device with OBD-dongle. Malicious device gets access to the OBD-Dongle.  Data, such as speed, engine output, boost, etc., can be read. However, there is no write access to the CAN bus attached to OBD.
