British police reported an increase in the number of car thefts in the Wilmslow and Alderley Edge areas. A number of models have been targeted, including Mercedes, BMW, Volkswagen, and Audi. In light of the concerning number of thefts, the police published suggestions for the public to protect their vehicles, specifically addressing vehicles with electronic car keys and advising owners to keep them in a security pouch to prevent them from being scanned by thieves.
